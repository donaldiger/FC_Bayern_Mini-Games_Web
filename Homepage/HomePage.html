Introducing FC Bayern München

FC Bayern München, commonly known as Bayern Munich, is Germany's most decorated and internationally renowned football club. Founded on February 27, 1900, by eleven football enthusiasts led by Franz John, Bayern has grown from a local team into one of the most dominant forces in world football.

Based in Munich, Bavaria, the club holds the record for the most Bundesliga titles and numerous DFB-Pokal victories. On the European stage, they have claimed the prestigious UEFA Champions League trophy multiple times.

The team plays at the Allianz Arena, a glowing red fortress on matchdays that holds over 75,000 fans. Known for their attacking style and powerhouse mentality, Bayern has been home to legends like Franz Beckenbauer, Gerd Müller, Thomas Müller and Manuel Neuer.

Beyond trophies, FC Bayern stands for tradition, fan loyalty, and community. Their motto, "Mia san mia", embodies unity, pride, and confidence.

Whether you're a lifelong supporter or just discovering the club, one thing is certain: FC Bayern München is not just a team—it's a legacy.
